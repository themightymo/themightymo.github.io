What To Expect In Our Contractor Coaching Sessions

This business-building stuff is messy business, and I really only have one qualification to working together: You need to be honest.  With yourself, with me, etc. 
 
I’ll do the same.
 
I have found 1-on-1 weekly mentoring sessions provide the most value, because they force us to regularly dive deep, think hard, and deliver.

My guidance will be based on your business objectives (which we’ll work together to develop) and capacity. 

I lean towards simple, pragmatic solutions rather than specific technology stacks.  

 

Investment & Commitment

Consider this: your future is already created. It’s called your default future. It’s the future that’s going to take place when no further thoughts, intentions or actions are brought to it.

Your default future is rarely discussed and so it is invisible. It determines all of your actions. Most people live into their default future. They constantly project that future into the present, and never get ahead. If you look at them today you will know where they will be five, ten, twenty years from now.

What does your gut tell you life will be like in ten years if you don’t change anything? Nothing. Same everything. Ten years from now – what will you be doing? What will life be like?

– Robert Dempsey

I ask that you wholeheartedly commit 3 months to our process together.  Changing habits take time, and 3 months is what is needed to discover and alter human habits.
 
You’re here, because you want to change some patterns in your life to achieve your biz/life goals.  That’ll be uncomfortable at times, and we’ll get through it together.
