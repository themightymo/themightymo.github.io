I Never Thought I’d See The Day When…

Jan 23, 2018 | Become a Calm Company, Blog, News

Realtors are widely-regarded as the most work-life unbalanced freelancers out there.

They work day and night and are available to close deals on a dime.

They are constantly in a race to return phone calls and emails.

That’s why today I was surprised to learn that my realtor friend took the day off.

It was a snow day for Minneapolis schools.

He had child care lined up so he didn’t have to take the day off.

But he did anyway.

I love it.

This is why he owns his own business.

This is what it’s all about.

He prioritizes his family and his business works around his family goals.

I love it.
